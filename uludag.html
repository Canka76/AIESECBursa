Winter Fun on Uludağ – Snow, Skiing & Cozy Cafes

When winter arrives, Uludağ transforms into a snowy wonderland that feels straight out of a postcard. Just a short drive or cable car ride from Bursa’s city center, the mountain is one of Turkey’s top ski resorts. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, there are plenty of slopes for skiing and snowboarding—and even if you don’t ski, there’s lots to enjoy. You can ride snowmobiles, go sledding, or just play in the snow! There are cozy cafés and hotels up there too, where you can warm up with a hot chocolate or traditional Turkish soup while enjoying the mountain views. It's perfect for a day trip or even a weekend stay

Summer on Uludağ – Fresh Air, Forest Walks & Picnics

In summer, Uludağ becomes a peaceful nature retreat. The snow melts away, revealing green forests, wildflowers, and cool mountain air. It’s a favorite spot for locals and visitors to escape the heat and enjoy picnics, hiking, or simply relaxing under the trees. There are easy walking trails for all ages, and if you're into photography, the views are stunning. Bring your own snacks or grab a bite at one of the mountain restaurants. Whether you're traveling solo, with friends, or as a family, Uludağ is a great way to reconnect with nature and recharge.
