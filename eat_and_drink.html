Eat & Drink in Bursa

Taste the tradition, savor the moment!

Discover Bursa's Culinary Delights!

From legendary kebabs to sweet treats and cozy cafés, Bursa is a paradise for food lovers. Explore the city's rich flavors and unique dining experiences.

İskender Kebab

İskender Kebab is Bursa's most famous dish, featuring thinly sliced döner meat over soft pita bread, topped with savory tomato sauce and sizzling melted butter. Served with creamy yogurt, it's a must-try for every visitor. Enjoy this legendary meal at one of the city's historic kebab houses!

Koza Han Café Experience

Relax in the historic Koza Han, the heart of Bursa's silk trade. Sip Turkish tea or coffee in the beautiful courtyard, surrounded by centuries-old architecture and the vibrant energy of local life. It's the perfect spot to unwind and soak up the city's unique atmosphere.

Local Sweets

Bursa is famous for its delicious sweets, especially candied chestnuts (kestane şekeri) and Turkish delight. Treat yourself to these local specialties at traditional sweet shops throughout the city, and bring home a taste of Bursa for your loved ones!
