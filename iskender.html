The Legendary Taste of Bursa: İskender Kebab

İskender Kebab is more than just a meal — it's a symbol of Bursa's rich culinary heritage. Made with thinly sliced doner meat layered over pieces of soft pita bread, it's generously topped with a savory tomato sauce and sizzling melted butter. Served with a side of cool, creamy yogurt, this dish is a flavorful journey you won't forget. Whether you're a foodie or just hungry after exploring, İskender Kebab is a must-try local favorite!

Where to Eat the Best İskender Kebab in Bursa

  • Kebapçı İskender (Tayyare Kültür Merkezi) – Run by the descendants of the original İskender Efendi, this is the birthplace of the iconic dish.
  • Kebapçı İskender (Heykel) – Another branch of the original family-owned business, located in the heart of the city.
  • Kebapçı İskender (Çekirge) – With a historic atmosphere and panoramic view, this location is a favorite among locals and tourists alike.
  • Uludağ Kebapçısı Cemal & Cemil Usta – Known for its high-quality meat and long tradition, this spot is a local classic.
  • Kebapçı Hüseyin – A hidden gem with great flavors and a cozy, authentic vibe.
