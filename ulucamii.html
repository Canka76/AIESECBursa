The Legendary Taste of Bursa: İskender Kebab

If you’re in Bursa, the Ulucami (Grand Mosque) is a must-visit spot that feels both peaceful and impressive. Unlike many mosques with just one big dome, this one has 20 smaller domes—making it look really unique and cool! When you step inside, you’ll notice beautiful calligraphy decorating the walls—like elegant artwork made from words. The mosque is right in the city center, so it’s easy to find and a great place to take a break from busy streets. It’s a calm, quiet spot where you can enjoy the calm atmosphere and soak up a bit of local culture.

Tips for Visiting and What to Do Nearby

When you visit Ulucami, remember to dress respectfully (cover your shoulders and legs) and take off your shoes before going inside. It’s a peaceful place, so try to keep your voice low and enjoy the calm vibes. After exploring the mosque, don’t miss the nearby Koza Han—a lively market just a few minutes away. Here you can find beautiful silk scarves, souvenirs, and enjoy a cup of Turkish tea in one of the cozy cafes. Visiting Ulucami is not just about seeing a building—it’s about feeling the calm and warmth of Bursa’s heart.
