What’s Koza Han All About?

Koza Han is one of Bursa’s most charming and lively spots—a beautiful old market right in the city center. Built centuries ago, it used to be the place where silk traders met, and today, it still carries that special vibe. Walking through its stone archways and cozy courtyards feels like stepping back in time, but with plenty of modern energy. Inside, you’ll find stalls full of colorful silk scarves, handmade crafts, and unique souvenirs. It’s a perfect place to pick up gifts or just wander around and soak up the atmosphere. Plus, the architecture is stunning, with peaceful fountains and comfy seating areas to relax

Visiting Tips and Fun Things to Do

When you visit Koza Han, take your time exploring the different shops—don’t be shy to chat with friendly shopkeepers who love sharing stories about their crafts. After shopping, treat yourself to a cup of traditional Turkish tea or coffee at one of the quaint cafes inside the courtyard. It’s the perfect way to rest and watch the world go by. Koza Han is lively during the day but especially magical in the late afternoon when the sun softens, and the market calms down a bit. It’s also a great spot to enjoy some people-watching or plan your next adventure around Bursa.
